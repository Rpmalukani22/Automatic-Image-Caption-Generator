What is Image Captioning?

Automatic image annotation (also known as automatic image tagging or linguistic indexing) is the process by which a computer system automatically assigns metadata in the form of captioning or keywords to a digital image. Automatically describing the content of an image is a fundamental problem in artificial intelligence that connects computer vision and natural language processing.

Here we are presenting a generative model based on a deep re-current architecture that combines recent advances in computer vision and machine translation and that can be used to generate natural sentences describing an image.
